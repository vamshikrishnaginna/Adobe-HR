‏Agenda‏‏ ‏

‏Day 1 :Entrepreneurship‏

‏Day 2: Getting started with android‏

‏Day 3:Playing with Layouts‏

‏Day 4:Real-Time Deployment‏

‏Day 5:Project Expo‏

‏Day 1: Entrepreneurship‏‏ ‏

‏On the day one we will have a talk on entrepreneurship. Discuss how can an individual start his own start up from scratch.Also speak about the journey of Ezerka like how did Ezerka actually start, how was our team formed, team members and team member roles , how did we get our first earnings, our first client and so on. We can also invite some other guest speaker or entrepreneur.‏

‏Day 2 : Getting Started with Android‏‏ ‏

‏When it comes to 2nd day of the series of workshop participants are exposed to basics of android like what are activities, layouts, buttons etc. It is to be noted that the installation part has to done by the student himself at home before coming to the workshop and required links will be shared at the end of day 1 workshop.Concluding the day with very basic android application like number counter. Before the workshoped is adjourned students are given a task as home work. ‏

‏Day 3 : Playing with Layouts‏

‏On day 3 when students come, they are asked to submit the homework and any errors are solved with the help of coordinators. Creating an android app with more than one activity, linking them and building a bit complex app.‏

‏Day 4: Real-Time Deployment‏

‏Introducing Firebase database to students, a small android app which can send data to database and fetch it. Finally teaching them how can you upload an app to Playstore.Give them all the resources , links , videos , softwares and what all is needed to make them become a android developer. Announce about project expo, pros of participating, benefits they can grab.‏

‏ ‏

‏Day 5: Project Expo‏‏ ‏

‏Final day of the series of workshop is project expo. Students with innovative ideas are invited to the expo with their projects. Here we need jury from outside and if required we can also participate in jury. After jury we can select top 3 teams or projects and decide their prizes or any other benefits we can provide like certificates or internships. At the end collect the feedback.‏
